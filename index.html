1. Information Collection and Use

Stack Hop is designed as an offline game. We do not require users to create an account or provide personal information (such as name, email, or phone number) to play the game.

  • Game Data: We collect non-identifiable game-play data such as high scores, current level, total blocks stacked, and unlocked skins. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.
  • Permissions: The app may request standard permissions required for game functionality, such as vibration (for haptic feedback).

2. Third-Party Services

While the game itself is offline, if the app is downloaded through the Google Play Store, Google may collect certain information as part of their service. We encourage you to review the Google Play Services Privacy Policy.

3. Children’s Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data at all, the game is safe for players of all ages.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. Since all game progress is stored locally on your device, your data is as secure as your mobile device itself. If you clear the app cache or uninstall the app, your game progress (scores and skins) will be permanently lost.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@stackhop.com
